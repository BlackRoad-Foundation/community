## Micro Grants ($500–$5,000)
For tools, libraries, and integrations that expand the BlackRoad OS ecosystem.

## Project Grants ($5,000–$50,000)
For larger projects that advance the mission of open, autonomous AI infrastructure.

## Application Process
1. Open a GitHub Discussion with title `[Grant] Project Name`
2. Describe the project, budget, and timeline
3. Foundation board reviews quarterly

## Priorities
- Agent infrastructure tooling
- Privacy-preserving AI systems
- Decentralized compute networks
- Open hardware integrations
